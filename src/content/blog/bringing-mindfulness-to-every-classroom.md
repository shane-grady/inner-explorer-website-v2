Mindfulness works best as a daily habit, not a one-time event. When students begin
the day with a short, guided practice, they arrive at learning calmer and more
focused — and teachers get a few minutes of calm too.

## Why daily matters

Consistency is what turns a practice into a skill. A few minutes each morning,
every day, gives students repeated chances to notice their attention wandering and
gently bring it back. Over a school year, those small moments add up.

## Built for the school day

Each practice is short, audio-guided, and ready to play — no preparation required.
Teachers press play; students follow along. That simplicity is what makes daily use
realistic across an entire school.
